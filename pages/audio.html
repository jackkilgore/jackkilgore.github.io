Dusk

Dusk is a music composition performed at the International Computer Music Conference 2019 and the New York City Electroacoustic Music Festival. A stereo piece using field recordings and granulation techniques. It has been officially released under the 20.8(3) microseconds album, "By the Broken Barn".
